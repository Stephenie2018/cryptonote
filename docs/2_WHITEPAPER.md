## 📄 The Whitepaper (Vision & Design)

Now that you have the basic tools, the second step is understanding the "Why" and "How" of your project. In the crypto world, this is done through a **Whitepaper**.

### 🔍 Why is a Whitepaper important?
Creating a whitepaper is a crucial exercise when building a cryptocurrency. It helps you:
* **Define your vision:** What problem are you solving?
* **Structure the economics:** How will your tokens or coins work?
* **Build trust:** It shows future users and developers that you have a solid plan.
* **Visual Identity:** It helps people form an image of what your project will eventually become.

> [!TIP]
> **Don't worry!** While it is very important for the final version of a project, **you can skip this step for now** if you just want to experiment with the code first. You can always come back later to write down your ideas.
